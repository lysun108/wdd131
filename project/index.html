Welcome

Your essential companion for planning a scenic and stress-free road trip to Yosemite National Park.

Yosemite sits in California’s Sierra Nevada and is famed for giant sequoias, sculpted granite like El Capitan and Half Dome, Tunnel View, and soaring waterfalls. Spring brings lush greens and peak flows; summers are blue-sky classics; fall glows golden; winters bring snow and quiet. [Source]
