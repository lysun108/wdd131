  • The Ahwahnee Bar: Indoor/outdoor seating year-round for drinks, hot cocoa, and snacks.
  • Curry Village Pizza Patio & Bar: Pizza, salads, and drinks for lunch and dinner. Seasonal hours with outdoor seating.

Lodging

Lodging inside the park is also operated by Yosemite Hospitality. Options range from simple tent cabins to deluxe rooms. Reservations open 366 days in advance and are highly recommended during peak seasons.

  • Yosemite Valley Lodge: Near Yosemite Falls with easy access to dining and attractions. Open year-round.
  • Wawona Hotel: Historic Victorian lodge near the Mariposa Grove of Giant Sequoias. Open seasonally.
